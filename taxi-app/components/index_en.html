How It Works

  1. Book your taxi with the date, time, number of passengers, and route defined. You'll have it ready in less than 5 minutes!
  2. Select the necessary extras that will make your trip even better and the payment method to use.
  3. 24 hours before your arrival, you will receive a confirmation message with your taxi driver's details to coordinate your pickup.
  4. When you land, the driver will be waiting for you with a sign with your name on it. There will be no confusion!
  5. Arrive at your destination quickly, comfortably and safely, enjoying the views and landscape of Tenerife during the journey. When the journey is complete, your card will be charged automatically or in cash if you so choose.
  6. When you have completed your journey, you can provide feedback to help us improve our service and share your experience with other users.
